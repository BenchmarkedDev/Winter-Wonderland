Event Information

Join us this winter as our park gets transformed into a magical Winter Wonderland once again! You'll have the chance to experience something very special, and forge some truly unforgettable memories in the process! We've been organising this event anually for over 30 years now, and with brand-new attractions arriving this year, there has never been a better time to visit!

Get lost in the incredible scenery as you explore the enchanted snowy meadows. Take a break at our world-famous bar, which is made entirely out of ice and has been masterfully hand-crafted by some of the worlds most famous architects! Visit Santa Claus, solve complex puzzles, sing your heart out in Karaoke; there's an endless list of things you can do!

No matter your age, we guarantee you that you will find something to get excited about here at the Winter Wonderland. Coming as a family? Coming Alone? No problem - There's plenty of fun to be had by all!
